About Me

A former finance and sports professional turned Software Engineer, I like how that sounds! I recently came to my senses and decided to become Software Engineer after years of researching and learning about the field and I’m happy I did! I was select from over 1,200 applicants to participate in an American Express sponsored Software Engineer Bootcamp and General Assembly. I know what you’re thinking, “she’s new” and your absolutely right, but my grit, determination and passion for solving problems makes up for what I lack in experience, so lets chat!
